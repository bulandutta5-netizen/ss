Program Overview

The Foundation course at Rise Coaching is designed specifically to help secondary school students build strong logic and knowledge in Science and Mathematics. It bridges the gap between standard school books and competitive level questions.

This program builds a base for students aiming for competitive entrance examinations like IIT-JEE, NEET, and CUET in their higher secondary years, while also preparing them for scholarship exams like NTSE and Olympiads.

Syllabus Breakdown

Number Systems, Algebra, Geometry, Mensuration, Probability, and logical arithmetic puzzles. Special sessions are dedicated to speed math tricks.
Fundamentals of Physics (Motion, Force, Light), Chemistry (Matter, Elements, Acids & Bases), and Biology (Cell structure, Plant & Animal systems).
Verbal & Non-Verbal reasoning, pattern detection, series completion, coding-decoding, and puzzle-solving models.
