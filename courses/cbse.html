Program Overview

Our CBSE Boards Preparation program is built to ensure students score high marks in their Class 10 and 12 Board examinations. The coaching style emphasizes absolute conceptual clarity followed by writing practice.

We provide chapter-wise mock test sheets, previous year board exam papers revisions, and regular performance review seminars to highlight writing style and score optimizations.

Syllabus Breakdown

Strict adherence to official NCERT / CBSE curriculum. Early syllabus completion by November to allow full months of revision mock test blocks.
Focus on step-by-step marking schemes, clear presentation, handwriting neatness, and timing allocations during final board exams.
Exhaustive series of full-syllabus mock exams in December and January, matching the exact format, difficulty, and duration of official boards.
