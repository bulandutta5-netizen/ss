Program Overview

The IIT-JEE preparation program is designed for students aiming to qualify for JEE Mains and Advanced. The course structure is intensely focus-oriented, introducing JEE concepts early.

We train students in problem-solving speed, numerical calculation shortcuts, and negative-marking prevention, supported by our computer-based weekly test engines.

Syllabus Breakdown

Kinematics, rotational dynamics, wave optics, electrostatics, electromagnetism, and modern atomic physics with advanced numericals.
Physical chemistry concepts (Equilibrium, Kinetics), organic mechanism reactions, and inorganic coordination chemistry details.
Calculus integration limits, coordinate geometry parabolas/ellipses, complex numbers, matrices, and probability systems.
