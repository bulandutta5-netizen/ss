Our Purpose

Empowering Minds,
Shaping Bright Futures

Founded in 2015, Rise Coaching Institute has grown from a local tutorial center to a premier educational hub, assisting thousands of students in securing excellent ranks in CBSE, JEE, and NEET examinations.

We firmly believe that every student has unique strengths. Our pedagogy focuses on unlocking their individual potential through patience, scientific study materials, and rigorous mock evaluations.

With branches in Surat and Latur, we bridge the gap between school curriculum standards and the competitive standards of national engineering and medical entrances.

Our Mission

To deliver quality education, facilitate concept clarity, and offer structured mental scaffolding to help students achieve their academic dreams.

Our Vision

To be a leading national coaching institution recognized for pedagogical innovation, comprehensive study materials, and consistent academic success.
